The '90s are back and better than ever. However, you don't need us to tell you that. Everything from bouncy hair to chic, preppy fashion styles is what we are all trying to recreate & we are here for it.

 

No one does bouncy curly hair like women in the 90s. They showed us how it was really done. We're here to tell you how you can achieve this effortless look in under 20 minutes (thank us later).

 

 A hairstyle that will transition from day to evening. This style is our go-to if you want something that will last a few days. Don't mind us looking like we have woken up in a salon. If you are blessed enough to have hair that holds a curl, then you are in for a real treat.

 

This is a look we swear by & have never looked back since. Call it a NELLE signature look, and we can't wait for you to jump on the bandwagon.

 

Here is how we create big bouncy curls using our most used tool..heated rollers.

 

We know hair wash day is planned in our calendar 2 months in advance. It takes serious scheduling, so it's best to wash your hair the day before for the ultimate curl.

 

PREP YOUR HAIR

Prep your hair with heat spray & shine spray. The secret to a perfect curl starts with the prep. If you have flat hair, we recommend adding a volumising spray to add that extra sass.

 

APPLY ROLLERS

Once the rollers have heated, separate your hair into sections. Apply your rollers to each section of hair. For a wavy curl, use larger sections of hair. If you prefer a tighter curl, apply to smaller segments close to the root. Use this time to make a start on your makeup or to prepare the cocktails.

 

LET THE ROLLERS WORK THEIR MAGIC

Leave your hair in the rollers until they feel cool to touch. Twenty minutes is our recommended time. Remove one roller first to see if you need to leave it any longer.

 

REMOVE

The fun part. Removing the rollers, don't worry. They will drop.

 

TOP TIP

If you have the time, once you have taken your rollers out, place them in pin curls & allow the hair to set for a couple of hours. Pin-curling your hair will provide longevity to your new go-to look, meaning you can flick your hair all night.

 

SET, SPRAY, PARTAY

Set the curls with hairspray to avoid the disappointment of them slowly dropping throughout the night (cries).

 

The blowdry-inspired look..without the blowdry.
